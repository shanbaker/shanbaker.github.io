Hi, I'm Shan -

a UX Designer based in in Northern NJ.


As a life-long creative, with over fifteen years in the professional music industry, I've done many things, such as composed original music for my 19-member progressive jazz orchestra, played saxophone in various bands and taught private music lessons to hundreds of students of various ages and backgrounds.

Along with music I've always had a fascination with design and technology. As I young child I was constantly drawing and writing to make sense of the world around me. As a teen I worked summers in my uncle's construction office and would often study and obsess over house plans that the architects would send. I also had a friend who's dad had a computer business. Often, after school I would often find myself in a garage helping them build PCs. More recently I've found myself creating websites for my various music projects and trying to decipher code from websites that I found interesting, useful or visually pleasing.

My passion for UX Design stems from my fascination with human psychology, communication, emotions, and energy. One aspect that I love about being a designer and a composer is the opportunity to emerge people into the flow of an experience. I enjoy the process and challenge of bridging the abstract connections between one’s internal and external world and always strive to bring this element to anything that I create.

I am also very passionate about human rights, animal rights and social justice. With the strong belief that design and technology can be leveraged to benefit those that are vulnerable or marginalized, I am constantly brainstorming ideas for apps and platforms that have the potential to better society.
